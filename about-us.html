About Us

Chronic cough is a common and often extremely disruptive condition that can cause sleep deprivation, muscle fatigue and social anxiety.

We are the only cough center in Islamabad/Rawalpindi and is one of few offering health services exclusively for chronic cough in the nation. Dr. Yaver Kazi has 25 years of diagnosing and effectively treating chronic coughers who had been referred to him by their primary care or specialty physician for what they thought was a cough caused by allergies or asthma. Cough Center is part of his commitment to advance the understanding, diagnosis and treatment of chronic cough.

Most health conditions that cause chronic cough are easily diagnosed. However, without expert knowledge and experience, some associations with coughing can be easily missed. We offer our patients the expertise to alleviate cough symptoms.

In many cases, we can identify the source of the problem from the symptoms alone, without diagnostic tests, and can recommend a course of treatment on a patient's first visit.
